About GoddessAlert

What you're recommending

GoddessAlert is a relationship app for men. It gives them one small, concrete tip every day — personalised to their weakest areas — along with smart reminders for important moments and a relationship health score based on what they actually do, not what they promise.

It's a conscious alternative to the manosphere. The foundation is attention and respect — not dominance, not princess treatment. Ten percent of profits go to women's equality organisations. Another ten percent to sustainability. Both committed from day one.

The tip is always a starting point. The user brings the judgment — he knows her, the context, and what she needs. GoddessAlert just helps him remember to show up.
