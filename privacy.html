2. What is GoddessAlert for?

GoddessAlert is a private application. You use it to:

  • Set reminders for important moments
  • Receive and rate daily attention tips
  • Track your own relationship health score
  • Manage personal to-dos
Important: GoddessAlert is intended exclusively for personal use by the registered user. The app is not accessible to third parties, including your partner.

3. What data do we process?

3.1 Your data (account holder)

Account data: email address, registration date, language preference.

Profile data (optional): age, cultural background, hobbies and interests, education level.

Usage data: completed reminders and to-dos, tip ratings, weekly check-in responses, login times, subscription status and payment history.

3.2 Data about your partner and other people in your life

GoddessAlert allows you to enter basic data about your partner and other women in your life (such as your mother, mother-in-law, or daughter). These are data about third parties who do not have an account with GoddessAlert.

We only process: first name, date of birth, anniversary dates and other personally indicated dates, and preferences and interests you enter.

We never process: contact details, financial data, health data, or other sensitive personal data (Art. 9 GDPR).

The legal basis for this processing is legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — comparable to maintaining a birthday calendar. We have conducted a formal balancing test and consider this use justified given the minimal amount of data, the exclusively private nature, and the strict security measures.

3.3 Payment data

Payments are processed via Stripe. GoddessAlert does not store complete payment details. See stripe.com/privacy for more information.

6. Who do we share your data with?

GoddessAlert never sells your data to third parties. We share data only with processors who process data on our behalf:

  • Supabase — database hosting and storage (EU servers)
  • Vercel — web application hosting
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Resend — email notifications
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI processing for personalised tips and content

All processors are bound by data processing agreements and process data in accordance with GDPR. We may provide data to government authorities if legally required to do so.

7. Is data processed outside the EU?

Some of our processors are located outside the European Economic Area (EEA). In such cases, we ensure an adequate level of protection through the use of European Commission-approved standard contractual clauses (SCCs) and recognised certification schemes.

8. Security of your data

Technical measures: encryption of data at rest and in transit (HTTPS), Row Level Security ensuring each user can only access their own data, magic link authentication without stored passwords, and regular security audits.

Organisational measures: minimal data access, confidentiality obligations, and an incident procedure for data breaches in accordance with GDPR requirements.

In the event of a data breach likely to affect your rights and freedoms, we will notify you within 72 hours in accordance with Article 34 GDPR.
